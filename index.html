Problem
Invisible on Google — an old site with nothing indexed, so high-intent local searches went to whoever showed up first.
Approach
Deep research to find where they could win, then built the pages to own those niches — Eichler-home glass, commercial storefronts, multi-family retrofits, property managers, a page per service-area city — each feeding a lead-capture form that routes jobs to the shop.
